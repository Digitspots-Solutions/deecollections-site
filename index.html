About Us

Deecollections is Port Harcourt's unique lifestyle destination that brings together two essentials – great food and great fashion. Located in the heart of Obio Akpor at No 24 Amaigbo Road, Mile 3 Diobu Ikokwu, we offer our customers a distinctive shopping and dining experience. Whether you're looking to enjoy a satisfying meal with family and friends or update your wardrobe with the latest fashion trends, Deecollections has you covered. Our restaurant serves a variety of delicious Nigerian and continental dishes prepared with fresh ingredients, while our clothing section features carefully curated fashion pieces for men, women, and children. We believe life is about enjoying the best of both worlds, and that's exactly what we deliver.

What We Offer

Restaurant Services

Our restaurant serves a delightful range of Nigerian favourites including jollof rice, pounded yam with assorted soups, grilled fish and chicken, as well as continental dishes. We cater to dine-in customers, takeaway orders, and special events. Every meal is prepared with care using quality ingredients to ensure your satisfaction.

Fashion Collection

Our clothing section features the latest trends in men's, women's, and children's fashion. From casual everyday wear to stylish outfits for special occasions, we stock quality pieces that suit the Nigerian lifestyle and climate. Whether you need corporate attire, traditional wear, or trendy streetwear, you'll find something perfect at Deecollections.

Special Events & Catering

We also provide catering services for your parties, meetings, and special occasions. Contact us to discuss your event needs and let us make your celebration memorable.
